Team Name

QTHD is the name we have chosen for our team. The reason behind this is that those uppercase letters are the first characters in our names: Quan, Tri, Huy, and Duy. Moreover, we intentionally put Huy and Duy at the and so we ended up having the letters “HD” at the end, which indicated our hard work so that we could be able to achieve that score at the end of this course, and the other courses in the future too.

Overall Processes

We were all pleased with how effectively we collaborated on producing the report and developing the website after submitting Assignment 2. However, because the structure of the report in Assignment 3 differs, our methodologies have been modified to better suit it. In the coming weeks, we will begin performing thorough research on our project concept, which is the VR headgear and the virtual environment within, in order to produce a more detailed report. To achieve that goal, we have decided to divide the team into two halves, each of which will handle a different area of the project:

Hardware Team

The hardware team including Quan and Tri will research for information on how to design and test the hardware components of the VR system.

Software Team

The software team which consists of Huy and Duy will be in charge of looking into how we can develop the software, backend systems and user interface.

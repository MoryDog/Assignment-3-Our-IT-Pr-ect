Because our career plans are very various, ranging from AI, Machine Learning, Games, to Software Engineering, and Mobile Development. Overall, the four of us maybe have unique roads to achieve, and inside this project, we can use our different knowledge to support each other and develop a super creative, colorful world inside the headset.
